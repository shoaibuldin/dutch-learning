The Lezen exam in one minute

Computer-based at a DUO exam centre · 65 minutes · about 25 questions on short everyday texts (gemeente letters, notices, ads, schedules, rules, short articles). Aim for ~19 of 25 (about 76%). No speaking or writing. Every answer is in the text — the real challenge is the trick options (strikvragen), so read each question carefully and find the proof sentence. (See the Strategy and Mock exam tabs.)

Note: under the 2021 inburgering law the default reading level is now B1; A2 applies if you fall under the older 2013 law or have been formally set to the A2 route — check your DUO letter if unsure.
